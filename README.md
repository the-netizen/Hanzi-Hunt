## The idea
Chinese is a deeply visual language — many characters started as drawings of the things they represent! So why must we limit its learning to flat text and textbook memorization when we can go on a hunt to discover the visual connection between some words and their meaning?

## The way it works
You point your camera at everyday objects around you, capture them, and collect their Chinese characters. Each captured word is saved to your personal gallery with the photo you took.

## Core flow
1. Open the camera and select an object from the scroll or search bar
2. The Chinese character floats on screen — point your camera at the real object
3. Tap capture, then trace the character on the word card with your finger- writing by hands aids memorization!
4. The word is then added to your gallery with your photo

## Built With
- SwiftUI + MVVM architecture
- AVFoundation for camera and photo capture
- 151 HSK-aligned vocabulary words embedded in JSON
- No third-party libraries used
